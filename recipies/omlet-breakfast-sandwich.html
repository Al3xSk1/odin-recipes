Omlet Breakfast Sandwich

Description

A classic egg and cheese omlet smacked between two breakfast breads

Ingredients

  • PAM cooking spray
  • Milk or water
  • 2 large eggs
  • Shredded cheese
  • 2 slices of a breakfast bread (bread, English muffin, bagel, etc...)

Procedure

Step 1

Turn on stovetop, set it to medium heat. Set the frying pan on the stovetop to heat it up. Crack the two large eggs into a bowl and a tad of milk/water into the bowl. Stir until it is mostly yellow. Cut the breakfast bread into two slices and are readied in the toaster. Spray the PAM cooking spray onto the frying pan.

Step 2

Pour the mixed eggs into the frying pan and cover it. Start to toast the breakfast breads. Sprinkle the shredded cheese in the frying pan and place the cover back on.

Step 3

Take the toasted breakfast breads out of the toaster and place them on a plate. Once the eggs are fully cooked fold the omlet into a half and then a quarter. Turn off the stovetop and place the omlet onto the bottom slice of the breakfast bread and then place the top on.
